    Get yourself
    Ready For exciting Activities

    The Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at COMSATS University Islamabad, Wah Campus, is thrilled to announce its upcoming Sports Week, a vibrant celebration of athleticism and camaraderie. This event, designed to engage students and faculty alike, will feature a diverse array of indoor and outdoor games, fostering teamwork and healthy competition.

    "Champions keep playing until they get it right."

    – Billie Jean King
    Join us for Sports Week, where we celebrate not only sports but also the spirit of community and collaboration at COMSATS University Islamabad, Wah Campus!

    Sports Week Announcement


    As we prepare for Sports Week, we invite all students and faculty to join in the festivities! This is a great opportunity to showcase athletic skills, build friendships, and create lasting memories.

    Team Sports:
    Compete in football, basketball, and volleyball, testing teamwork and strategy. Individual Competitions: Challenge yourself in athletics, swimming, and tennis for medals and glory. Fun Activities: Participate in tug-of-war, sack races, and relay races for some lighthearted fun. Additionally, we’ll host workshops and motivational talks from experienced athletes to promote physical fitness and sportsmanship.

    Mark your calendars!
    Whether you’re an athlete or just looking to have fun, there’s something for everyone. Let’s come together to cheer on our teams and celebrate our love for sports! COMSATS University Islamabad, Wah Campus!

    About Us

    History


    COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI), Wah Campus, was established in 2001 as part of the COMSATS Institute of Information Technology, aiming to provide quality higher education in science and technology. Located in the picturesque Wah Cantt, the campus is dedicated to fostering academic excellence and innovation. Over the years, it has evolved into a leading institution, contributing significantly to research and development in various fields.

    Standards


    We adhere to the highest educational standards, promoting a culture of research, critical thinking, and innovation. Our faculty comprises experienced educators and industry professionals, committed to mentoring students and guiding them toward success. We emphasize the importance of extracurricular activities, encouraging students to engage in sports, cultural events, and community service, fostering well-rounded development.

    Current Studies


    At Wah Campus, we offer a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across disciplines, including Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, Business Administration, and Environmental Sciences. Our curriculum is designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ensuring they are well-prepared for the challenges of the modern workforce. With state-of-the-art laboratories, libraries, and learning resources, we provide an enriching academic environment..

    As we look to the future,
    COMSATS Wah Campus continues to strive for excellence, aiming to be a hub of learning and innovation that produces capable leaders and responsible citizens.
